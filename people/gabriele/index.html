Short BIO

Gabriele Costa is a researcher at University of Genova. He received his Master degree (2006) and PhD (2011) and at the University of Pisa. From 2007 to 2011 he had a position as researcher at the Security Group of the Italian National Research Council. He was actively involved in several national national and european projects including:
NESSoS
Network of Excellence on Engineering Secure Future Internet Software Services and Systems.
Aniketos
Ensuring Trustworthiness and Security in Service Composition.
SPaCIoS
Secure Provision and Consumption in the Internet of Services.
Connect
Emergent Connectors for Eternal Software Intensive Networked Systems.

His research interests include, but are not limited to:

  • Language-based and history-based security frameworks
  • Formal methods for the static an dynamic verification of mobile applications
  • Security assessment of mobile and embedded systems
  • Automatic techniques for contract-based secure service composition
